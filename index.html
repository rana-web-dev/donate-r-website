The situation of the flooded people

The situation in Sylhet is dire due to the rising flood waters. Many houses have been submerged. They have no food and no provision for food.

Especially in some areas like Sylhet and Sunamganj the water level has risen so much that there is no limit to human suffering. The amount of water is increasing every day due to rains.



How to help them?

They are looking at us

If we don't help them, they will starve to death, so we should help them. They are waiting for us. We should stand by their side as much as possible.

If we stand by them, at least they will not die without eating. Not only people are helpless in the flood waters, all kinds of animals and birds have no shelter, there is no food, they are dying without eating.
